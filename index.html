Nicholas Scott Moline is a Senior Software Engineer at Justia, a company in Mountain View, California dedicated to making the law free and available for everyone. At Justia Nick works on a variety of free legal information projects with the rest of the talented engineering team there.

Check out Nick Moline's blog to find out more about him. He occasionally also writes on Justia's Blog. He can also be found on twitter as @nickmoline, as well as on Facebook and LinkedIn.

Nick currently lives in Sunnyvale, California with his wife Barbara Moline, and their two cats Jax and Joules.
